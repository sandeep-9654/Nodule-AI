3D Convolutional Model

12
  • The Foundation: The U-Net architecture perfectly concatenates deep semantic (meaning) features with shallow spatial (location) features via skip connections.
  • The Weight Problem: Standard 3D convolutions require a catastrophic amount of FLOPs, completely halting edge devices.
  • Our Innovation: Implemented Depthwise Separable 3D Convolutions—splitting the calculation into spatial filtering and 1x1x1 pointwise feature combination.
  • The Result: Semantic volumetric segmentation achieved with only 154,812 parameters.

How 3D U-Net Works?

13

The symmetric U-Net divides the 3D grid into distinct semantic contracting and expanding branches. It predicts voxel geometries, confidence probabilities, and margins. Depthwise separable convolutions divide the calculations into spatial channels, slashing hardware dependencies.

By heavily utilizing Skip Connections, spatial edges are perfectly preserved resulting in high-detail anatomical reproduction without immense VRAM limits.

Implementation of 3D U-Net in the Project

14

This project implants the compressed 3D U-Net specifically for instantaneous inference localized entirely on consumer CPUs. The pipeline receives 64x64x32 patches representing complex anatomy from LUNA-22.

Generating spatial outputs simultaneously with probability logic means a physician dynamically maps both the shape of the tumor boundary and the numeric urgency instantaneously.

Malignancy Classification and Probability Logic

16

> 75% Probability

System automatically warns of irregular spiculated margins and officially recommends immediate PET-CT and biopsy correlation.

50-75% Probability

Notes indeterminate growth characteristics. System recommends cautious short-interval (3-6 month) follow-up CT scans based on Fleischner criteria.

< 25% Probability

Identifies smooth density. Recommends standard routine follow-up with extremely low malignancy risk.
